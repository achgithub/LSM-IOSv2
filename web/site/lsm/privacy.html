Privacy Policy

Last Stand Manager (Sports Manager) — last updated 3 July 2026

Last Stand Manager ("the app") is published by Sports Manager. It includes two game modes — Last Man Standing pools and Predictor prediction leagues — both covered by this one policy. This policy explains what data the app collects and why.

No account, no sign-in

The app does not require you to create an account or sign in. By default, your games, players, picks and predictions are stored locally on your device and are not sent to us. If you switch on the optional Cloud Backup, Cloud Publish or player-link features described below, some of that data leaves your device — see those sections for details.

League data

Fixtures, scores, standings and team data are fetched from our server, which in turn sources data from football-data.org. These requests don't include any personal information about you — they're anonymous reads of public sports data.

Cloud Backup (optional, paid tiers)

On the 3+ league subscription tiers, you can turn on Cloud Backup to protect your games against a lost or replaced phone. When you do, the app uploads a snapshot of the game(s) you choose — including player names, picks, predictions and results — to our storage (Cloudflare R2), identified by a restore code generated on your device. Your device also holds a persistent identifier which is sent with backup requests so we can find and retire old backups (we keep a small number of your most recent backups per device, not an unlimited history). Anyone with your restore code can restore that data to a device, so treat it like a password. Turning Cloud Backup off, or deleting the app, does not delete backups already stored with us — email us if you'd like a backup removed.

Cloud Publish (Predictor standings pages, optional, paid tiers)

For Predictor leagues, the 3+ league tiers let you publish a standings page that your players can view on the web — recent results, the league table and each player's points. You choose what to publish; nothing is published automatically. The page sits behind a PIN that you set and share separately from the link, and the page itself is only reachable via an unguessable web address. Player names are only shown to someone who has both the link and the correct PIN.

Player links and submissions (PWA, optional, paid tiers)

You can send individual players a personal link they can save to their phone's home screen to submit their own picks or predictions each round, instead of you entering them by hand. When a player submits through that link, their name and pick/prediction are sent to our server and held temporarily so you can review and approve them in the app; approved submissions are then copied into your local game data. We keep a rolling window of recent submissions on our server (older ones are pruned once a round is a few rounds old) and don't otherwise use this data. You can revoke a player's link at any time from within the app, which invalidates it.

Data availability between seasons

For each league, there is normally a short window of around one to two weeks between seasons (typically over the summer) while the new season's fixtures, teams and tables are being transferred onto our server. During this window the app may only be able to show the most recently cached data for that league rather than fresh updates. This doesn't affect games already in progress, and normal updates resume once the new season's data is in place.

Advertising

Free-tier use of the app shows ads served by Google AdMob. Depending on your consent choice (shown via Google's User Messaging Platform on first launch in the UK/EEA, and via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt elsewhere), AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to show relevant ads, or non-personalised ads if you decline. You can change your tracking choice at any time in your device's Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. See Google's Privacy Policy for details on what AdMob collects.

Subscriptions

Paid tiers (No Ads / 3 Leagues / 5 Leagues / 7 Leagues) are sold as auto-renewing subscriptions through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Cloud Backup, Cloud Publish and player links are bundled into the 3+ league tiers at no extra charge — there's no separate cloud purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple and managed on our behalf by RevenueCat, which receives an anonymous purchase/subscription identifier to validate your purchase and restore it across devices — we never see your card details. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy and Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and we don't knowingly collect data from them.

Your choices

  • Deleting the app removes all locally-stored game data, but not any Cloud Backup or published pages already stored with us — email us to have those removed.
  • You can revoke a player's submission link at any time from within the app.
  • You can opt out of personalised ads at any time via your device's tracking settings.
  • To cancel a subscription, use the App Store under your Apple ID → Subscriptions.
